Company Profile

Rizz Realty, Brokerage, & Construction is a dynamic, multi-service company based in Dumaguete City and Siquijor, dedicated to shaping communities through trusted real estate services and quality construction.

We specialize in real estate development, property brokerage, and residential and commercial construction, offering a seamless experience for buyers, sellers, and investors alike. Whether its helping clients find their dream home, managing property transactions, or building modern, sustainable spaces. Rizz delivers with integrity, reliability, and local expertise.
